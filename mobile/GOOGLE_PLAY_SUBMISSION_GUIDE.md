## STEP 1: Open Google Play Console

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: https://play.google.com/console
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. If this is your first time, you need to pay the $25 one-time registration fee

---

## STEP 2: Create New App

1. Click the blue "Create app" button in the top right
2. Fill in the form:

**App name:**
```
TIME APEX
```

**Default language:** English (United States) - en-US

**App or game:** Select "App"

**Free or paid:** Select "Free"

3. Check ALL the declaration boxes:
   - Check "Developer Program Policies"
   - Check "US export laws"

4. Click "Create app"

## STEP 3: Set Up Your App

After creating, you'll be in your app's dashboard. Complete these sections:

### 3A. Go to "Dashboard"
Look at the left sidebar. You'll see tasks to complete.

## STEP 4: Create Internal Testing Release

1. In left sidebar, click **Testing** then **Internal testing**
2. Click "Create new release"
3. Under "App bundles", click "Upload"
4. Select the AAB file: `application-2d09f050-0f4c-4307-ad9d-d07ffbd8a741.aab`
5. Wait for upload to complete (may take 1-2 minutes)
6. Once uploaded, scroll down to "Release notes"
7. Click "Add release notes" and select "English (United States) - en-US"
8. In the text box, paste EXACTLY this (no bullet points, plain text):

```
Initial release of TIME APEX - AI Trading and Bot Platform.

Features included:
AI-powered trading bot creation
Real-time portfolio tracking
Multi-broker integration
Trading signals and alerts
Biometric security (fingerprint and face recognition)
```

9. Click "Save"
10. Click "Review release"
11. If there are warnings (yellow), you can proceed. If there are errors (red), fix them first.
12. Click "Start rollout to Internal testing"
13. Click "Rollout" to confirm

## AFTER SUBMISSION

### Timeline
- Google Play review: 1-7 days (usually 2-3 days)
- You'll receive email updates

### If Rejected
1. Read the rejection email carefully
2. Fix the issues mentioned
3. Resubmit

### Common Rejection Reasons
- Missing privacy policy
- Incomplete store listing
- Data safety form incomplete
- Screenshots don't match app
- Metadata policy violations (don't use trademarked terms)

---

## RELEASE TRACKS EXPLANATION

- **Internal testing**: Only you and invited testers (up to 100)
- **Closed testing**: Invite-only, more testers
- **Open testing**: Anyone can join from store listing
- **Production**: Live on Google Play for everyone

Start with Internal, then promote to Production when ready.
